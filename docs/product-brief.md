# Product Brief

## Name

MedShelf

## One-Line Pitch

A safety-first medicine tracker that reads, translates, and simplifies medication leaflets into reviewed dose plans and inventory alerts.

## Problem

Medicine instructions are often hard to understand, especially when leaflets are long, written in another language, or filled with dense medical warnings. Existing phone health apps can track medication schedules, but they usually do not help users interpret the documentation that comes with the medicine.

## Target Users

- People taking recurring medicine
- Caregivers managing medicine for family members
- Travelers using medicine purchased in another country
- Multilingual households
- People who want a lightweight medicine inventory tracker

## Core User Story

As a user, I want to upload a medicine leaflet, get a translated and simplified explanation, review it, and turn it into a schedule so I can take medicine more confidently and avoid running out.

## Differentiator

The app does not merely track reminders. It connects three moments:

1. Understanding the medicine leaflet.
2. Turning reviewed instructions into a usable plan.
3. Tracking doses and supply over time.

## MVP Promise

In the demo, a judge should be able to add a medicine, upload a leaflet, review AI-extracted guidance, track a dose, and see stock/restock behavior in under three minutes.

## Non-Goals For MVP

- No diagnosis.
- No personalized medical recommendation.
- No automatic medication interaction advice unless grounded in uploaded/source text.
- No real payment or ordering flow.
- No HIPAA-grade production health record storage.
